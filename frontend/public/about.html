About Us

Who We Are

A Digital Notebook for Everyday Business Owners

FinLite is a people-first financial tool built for African small businesses to easily track daily sales, expenses, and profit. Designed around real shop owners, market traders, and POS operators, it reflects how local businesses actually work. By removing complex fintech interfaces, FinLite delivers a clear, familiar, and practical experience that helps everyday business owners stay organized, make better decisions, and run their businesses with confidence. confidence.

FinLite was built with them in mind. Real people. Real businesses. Real solutions.

Our Mission

To provide a stress-free way for business owners to record sales, track expenses, and see profit instantly.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and simplest financial companion for everyday business people.
